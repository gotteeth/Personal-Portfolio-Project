About Me

Profile

I'm Alex Santana, a passionate Dental Assistant with over 10 years of experience. My expertise includes 3D printing, 3D scanning, and CAD/CAM technologies. I am RSH certified and hold CPR/AED credentials. My adaptability to different work methods and my commitment to continuous learning in the dental field have been key to my success.

Background

My journey in dental assistance started over a decade ago, and since then, I've worked with some amazing teams and wonderful patients. At North Haven Dentists, I've explained procedures and guided patients through timelines and expectations, forming genuine connections and assisting with payment plans. My experience with 3D printing and scanning for dental mold creation has been particularly rewarding.

Education

I hold a High School Diploma from Central High School, earned in 2012. In addition to my formal education, I continually seek out opportunities to learn and grow within the dental field, attending workshops and certifications to stay at the forefront of technology and techniques.

Career Goals

I am currently seeking a full-time position that complements my part-time studies in science. My aim is to grow and contribute to a progressive dental office, where I can apply my skills and experience to provide exceptional patient care and support.

Personal Interests

Outside of work, I enjoy staying active and engaging in various outdoor activities. I have a keen interest in technology, which extends to my hobby of exploring new advancements in dental care. Additionally, I am an avid gamer and enjoy listening to podcasts, particularly those related to tech and personal development.
